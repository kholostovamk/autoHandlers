The 2023 Jeep Compass is a compact SUV that has been significantly updated for the new model year. It features refreshed exterior styling and a modernized interior with new technology and premium materials. The Compass is available with two powertrain options, including a new plug-in hybrid that provides impressive fuel economy and all-electric driving range. The hybrid system also delivers plenty of power and torque, making the Compass feel quick and responsive on the road. The Compass offers comfortable and spacious seating for up to five passengers, as well as plenty of cargo space. It also boasts impressive off-road capability, with available features such as a Selec-Terrain traction management system and Jeep's Active Drive Low 4x4 system. Overall, the 2023 Jeep Compass is a well-rounded compact SUV that offers a blend of on-road refinement, fuel efficiency, and off-road capability.
The 2023 Chevrolet Colorado gets an update, with new styling, powertrains, and technologies. The exterior features a redesigned grille, front fascia, and LED lighting. The interior also sees a refresh, with a new infotainment system and digital instrument cluster. The base engine is now a 2.5-liter four-cylinder, while a 2.7-liter four-cylinder and a 3.6-liter V-6 are also available. The ZR2 off-road package offers even more capability with features like Multimatic DSSV dampers and skid plates. The new Colorado also includes safety features such as automatic emergency braking and lane-departure warning. However, some reviewers find the new engine options to be underwhelming, and the Colorado's towing capacity is lower than some of its rivals. Overall, the 2023 Chevrolet Colorado offers some improvements over its predecessor, but may not stand out in a competitive midsize truck market.
The 2023 Honda Accord Touring is a midsize sedan with a comfortable ride and spacious interior. It is powered by a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine, which produces 252 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque, and is paired with a 10-speed automatic transmission. The Accord Touring gets an EPA-estimated fuel economy of 22 mpg in the city and 32 mpg on the highway. It comes standard with a suite of advanced safety features, including forward collision warning, automatic emergency braking, and lane departure warning. The Accord Touring also offers a variety of luxury features, such as heated and ventilated front seats, a head-up display, and a wireless charging pad. Its infotainment system features an 8-inch touchscreen display, wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto compatibility, and a 10-speaker premium audio system. The Accord Touring is a solid choice for those seeking a reliable and comfortable midsize sedan with plenty of features.
The Genesis G90 has been named the 2023 Car of the Year by MotorTrend, a popular automotive magazine. This is the first time that Genesis has won the award. The G90 was recognized for its excellent value proposition, refined luxury, and advanced technology features. The car's interior design and quality were also praised by the judges. The G90 is equipped with a powerful V8 engine, which delivers smooth and effortless acceleration. The car's ride and handling were described as refined and comfortable, and its advanced safety features were also noted. The G90 beat out stiff competition from other luxury brands, including Audi, BMW, and Mercedes-Benz, to win the award. Finally, the judges praised the G90 for its competitive pricing, which makes it an attractive alternative to more expensive luxury cars.
